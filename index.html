Privacy Policy — Invoice Manager

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Invoice Manager ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Invoice Manager mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address — used for account authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Invoice and client data — business data you enter manually
  • Expense data — financial records you create in the app
  • Profile information — name, company, tax ID, logo (optional)
  • Device token (FCM) — used for push notifications only

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality (invoices, clients, expenses, contracts)
  • To send push notifications about invoice status and due dates
  • To generate PDF invoices and Excel reports for your use
  • To send payment reminder emails to your clients (only when you request it)

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase services (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage) hosted on Google's infrastructure. Data is protected by Firebase security rules — only you can access your own data. We do not store any data on our own servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, database, and file storage
  • Resend — for sending payment reminder emails to your clients (only when you explicitly trigger this feature)

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

  • You can view and edit all your data within the app at any time
  • You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • You can disable push notifications in your device settings at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
